Is BabyCareX really 100% free?
Yes! BabyCareX is completely free forever. No hidden charges, no premium features, no ads. All features are completely free for all users!
Can I use it without internet?
Yes! BabyCareX works completely offline. You can track health, vaccines, and milestones without any internet. Data automatically syncs when you go online.
Is my baby's data safe?
Absolutely! Your data is encrypted with military-grade AES-256 encryption. We never share your personal information. Your privacy is always protected.
Which vaccines are tracked?
We track all 12 recommended vaccines from birth to 5 years: BCG, Hepatitis B, Polio, DPT, Measles & Rubella, and boosters - following WHO and IAP guidelines.
Can I track multiple children?
Yes! You can create profiles for multiple children and track each one independently. Perfect for families with multiple kids of different ages.
Is the app available in my language?
BabyCareX is available in English and Bengali. Easy language switching in settings. More languages coming soon based on user feedback!
Can health workers and doctors use it?
Yes! Healthcare professionals, doctors, nurses, and health workers can use BabyCareX for patient health record management and vaccination tracking.
Can I export and backup my data?
Yes! You can export all your health records and vaccination history. Data is automatically backed up to secure cloud storage for safety.
