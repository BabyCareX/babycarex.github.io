Common Questions

Is BabyCareX really free? ‚ñº
Yes! BabyCareX is completely free with no hidden costs or premium features. We believe every parent deserves access to quality health tracking tools. We don't show ads or sell your data - we're funded by community support.
Does it work without internet? ‚ñº
Yes! BabyCareX stores all your data locally on your device, so it works perfectly offline. You can add records, check vaccination schedules, and view growth charts anytime - even without internet connection.
Is my baby's health data secure? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption for all data. Your information is stored securely on your device and never shared with third parties. We don't track users or show targeted ads. Your family's privacy is our top priority.
Can I sync data across devices? ‚ñº
Yes! We offer optional cloud backup that you can enable. This allows you to sync your baby's health records across multiple devices securely. You control whether to use this feature or keep data local only.
Which age group is the app for? ‚ñº
BabyCareX is designed for babies aged 0-5 years. It includes age-appropriate vaccination schedules, developmental milestones, and growth charts specifically calibrated for this age group using WHO standards.
Does the app replace doctor consultations? ‚ñº
No. BabyCareX is a tracking and management tool to help you stay organized. It helps you remember appointments and track health information, but always consult your pediatrician for medical advice and diagnoses.
What languages are supported? ‚ñº
Currently, BabyCareX is available in English and Bengali with complete translations of all features, content, and health information. More languages are planned for future releases.
Can I export my baby's health records? ‚ñº
Yes! You can export vaccination records, growth charts, and health history in PDF or CSV format. This makes it easy to share with doctors or keep personal backups.
